About us

Ciel d'Or is the creation of leading Tunisian restaurant group, and Executive Chef Sookie st james. One of the country's most celebrated restaurants, Ciel d'Or has received Three Hats in the Good Food Guide for 8 consecutive years. The restaurant is an organic space reflective of Sookie St. James nature inspired cuisine.


Meet our chef

Our head chef Sookie St. James is a Creative and passionate chef with over 15 years of experience crafting innovative, award-winning dishes. Known for a meticulous approach to ingredient selection, dynamic leadership in kitchen management, and an unyielding enthusiasm for culinary perfection. She used to be the head chef of the Independence Inn and is the co-owner of the and chef at the dragonfly inn
